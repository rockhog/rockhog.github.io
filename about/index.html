About

Jun 16th, 2014

Rock Hog Drilling Products is a family owned business with over 40 years of manufacturing experience. With a 45,000 square foot manufacturing facility located in south central Pennsylvania, Rock Hog enjoys the best of all worlds. While we are located in a rural farmland community where work ethic of our people adds to our capability and value, we are also close to major interstate arteries and commerce I-70, I-76 and I-81. We are located just off Route 16, between Interstate 81 and the Pennsylvania Turnpike, in the borough of Mercersburg. The facility is easily accessible by our customers, suppliers and employees and welcome all to visit.
